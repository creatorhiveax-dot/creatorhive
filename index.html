Questions

Answered honestly.

What budget do I need to start?

Meaningful micro-influencer campaigns in India can start from around ₹25,000–₹50,000 in creator fees. On the discovery call we'll tell you honestly what your budget can and cannot achieve — before you spend anything.

How do you choose creators?

We look at audience fit first: who actually follows the creator, how they engage, and whether they match your customer. Follower count comes last. We also check for fake engagement before shortlisting anyone.

What do you charge?

A transparent management fee on top of creator costs — you always see exactly what creators are paid. No hidden markups. Exact pricing depends on campaign size, and we'll share it upfront on the call.

Do you work with small or new brands?

Yes — startups and early D2C brands are exactly who we're built for. Smaller budgets usually mean micro and nano creators, which often deliver better engagement per rupee anyway.

How fast can a campaign go live?

Typically 1–2 weeks from the discovery call to first post, depending on creator availability and how quickly content gets approved on your side.
